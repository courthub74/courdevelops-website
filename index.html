From design to development, I make workflow management and gig work/freelance applications. My aim is to help those in tech learn, stay organized, and get paid while they work to build professional qualifications. Adaptive web design and easy navigation for the user ranks high on my list of importance.

Ten years in tech has given me a first hand perspective in making effective applications. I do believe that mobile/web applications are the perfect idea for certain tasks in business and in life. The beauty is that they can be made customizable for each business and/or person. This portfolio was developed using good ole' HTML, SASS and JavaScript.
